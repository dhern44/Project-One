Lorelei's Team

Most of Lorelei's team has the ice typing accompanied with many ice type moves. This might lead you to believe that a fire type would be great here. Unfortunately 3 out of the 4 ice pokemon have a dual typing with water, negating the 2x effectiveness of fire moves.
With that being said, the best counter to Lorelei's chilling power would a fighting type pokemon. A fighting type, like Machamp for example, would deal 2x damage to 4/5 of her pokemon, making this fight a breeze!
After making quick work Lorelei, you are now ready for Bruno.
